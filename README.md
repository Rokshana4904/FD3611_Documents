# FD3611 Ethics and Approval Documents

This folder contains the official **ethical approval and institutional permission documents** supporting the **FD3611: A Multi-Class Color Fundus Image Dataset for Retinal Disease Classification**.

---

## Dataset Brief Description

FD3611 is a clinically validated color fundus image dataset designed for automated retinal disease classification and screening research. The dataset consists of **3,611 de-identified fundus images from 2,270 patients**, collected under routine diagnostic conditions at the **National Institute of Ophthalmology and Hospital, Dhaka, Bangladesh**.

The images are categorized into five diagnostic classes: **Diabetic Retinopathy, Media Hazy, Myopic Retinopathy, Optic Disc Disorder, and Normal**. All images were manually reviewed and validated by expert ophthalmologists, and natural variations in image quality were preserved to reflect real-world clinical conditions.

---

## Documents Included

- **Director_Approval.pdf**  
  Official permission letter issued by the Director of the National Institute of Ophthalmology & Hospital, Dhaka, Bangladesh, authorizing the collection and research use of retinal fundus images.

- **Ethical_Application.pdf**  
  Institutional Review Board (IRB) approval document issued by the Research Ethics Committee (REC) of the Faculty of Science and Information Technology, Daffodil International University.  
  **Ethics Approval Number:** REC-FSIT-2024-08-19

## Ethics Compliance

All data collection and secondary use were conducted in accordance with institutional ethical standards and international data protection guidelines. All patient data were fully anonymized prior to public release.
